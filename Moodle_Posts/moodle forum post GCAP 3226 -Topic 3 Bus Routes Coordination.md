# GCAP 3226 - Topic 3: Bus Routes Coordination

**Course**: [[GCAP3226 01 Empowering Citizens through Data- Participatory Policy Analysis for Hong Kong]]  
**Project Type**: Urban Transport Optimization  
**Group Size**: 4-6 students

## Project Overview
Analyzing potential for coordinating schedules between competing bus routes (272A-KMB and 582-CityBus) sharing 8 overlapping stops to improve passenger experience and system efficiency.

## Key Research Questions
- How can competing bus routes be better coordinated?
- What are the benefits of synchronized scheduling?
- How do passengers experience overlapping route services?
- What policy frameworks could facilitate coordination?

## Foundation Available
- Route overlap analysis for 272A-KMB and 582-CityBus
- 8 shared stops identified
- Schedule coordination potential assessment
- Passenger experience optimization opportunities

## Project Deliverables
- Route coordination analysis
- Schedule synchronization proposals
- Passenger impact assessment
- Policy implementation framework
