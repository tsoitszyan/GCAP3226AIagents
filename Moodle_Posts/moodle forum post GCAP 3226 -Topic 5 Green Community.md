# GCAP 3226 - Topic 5: Green Community Development

**Course**: [[GCAP3226 01 Empowering Citizens through Data- Participatory Policy Analysis for Hong Kong]]  
**Project Type**: Environmental Policy and Community Development  
**Group Size**: 4-6 students

## Project Overview
Analyzing green community initiatives and sustainable development practices to understand effectiveness of environmental policies and community engagement strategies in Hong Kong.

## Key Research Questions
- What makes green community initiatives successful?
- How can environmental policies be better implemented at community level?
- What role does citizen participation play in green development?
- How can data inform sustainable community planning?

## Foundation Available
- Green community case studies
- Environmental policy frameworks
- Community engagement methodologies
- Sustainability metrics and assessment tools

## Project Deliverables
- Green initiative effectiveness analysis
- Community engagement strategy assessment
- Policy implementation recommendations
- Sustainable development framework
